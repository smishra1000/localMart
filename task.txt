Backend Stories
User Authentication & Authorization

Story: As a user, I want to be able to create an account so that I can access the platform.
Story: As a user, I want to log in and log out of my account securely.
Story: As a user, I want to reset my password if I forget it.
Story: As an admin, I want to manage user roles (buyer, vendor) so that permissions are controlled.

Vendor Management

Story: As an admin, I want to approve or reject vendor registration requests.
Story: As a vendor, I want to create and manage my profile, including contact information and business details.
Story: As a vendor, I want to manage my product listings, including adding, editing, and removing products.
Product Catalog

Story: As a user, I want to browse a catalog of natural products categorized by type.
Story: As a vendor, I want to manage my product inventory, including updating stock levels and prices.
Story: As a user, I want to search for products by name, category, or vendor.
Order Management

Story: As a user, I want to add products to my cart and place an order.
Story: As a vendor, I want to receive notifications of new orders and manage the fulfillment process.
Story: As a user, I want to track the status of my orders.
Story: As a user, I want to receive order confirmation and delivery updates via email.
Payment Processing

Story: As a user, I want to pay for my orders securely through various payment methods.
Story: As a vendor, I want to receive payments and manage my earnings.
Story: As an admin, I want to manage the platform's transaction fees and payouts to vendors.
Review & Rating System

Story: As a user, I want to leave a review and rating for a product I purchased.
Story: As a vendor, I want to view and respond to reviews left on my products.
Story: As a user, I want to see reviews and ratings of products before purchasing.
Notification System

Story: As a user, I want to receive notifications about order status, promotions, and important updates.
Story: As a vendor, I want to receive notifications about new orders and customer inquiries.
Admin Dashboard

Story: As an admin, I want to monitor platform activity, including sales, user registrations, and vendor performance.
Story: As an admin, I want to generate reports on sales, transactions, and platform usage.


Frontend Stories

User Registration & Login

Story: As a user, I want a responsive and intuitive registration and login form.
Story: As a user, I want to be able to reset my password through the frontend.
Home Page

Story: As a user, I want to see a homepage that highlights featured products, categories, and promotions.
Story: As a user, I want to easily navigate to different sections of the site, such as product categories and vendor profiles.
Product Listing & Search

Story: As a user, I want to browse and filter products by category, price, and rating.
Story: As a user, I want to search for products using a search bar with autocomplete functionality.
Product Detail Page

Story: As a user, I want to see detailed information about a product, including images, description, price, and reviews.
Story: As a user, I want to add products to my cart from the product detail page.
Cart & Checkout

Story: As a user, I want to view the items in my cart, adjust quantities, and remove items.
Story: As a user, I want to proceed to checkout, enter my shipping information, and complete payment.
Order History & Tracking

Story: As a user, I want to view my order history and track the status of my orders.
Story: As a user, I want to receive a confirmation screen after placing an order, with an order summary and tracking link.
Vendor Profile

Story: As a user, I want to view vendor profiles, including their product offerings and contact information.
Story: As a vendor, I want to edit my profile information through a user-friendly interface.
Review & Rating

Story: As a user, I want to leave a review and rating for a product I purchased directly from the product page.
Story: As a user, I want to view reviews and ratings on product pages.
User Account Management

Story: As a user, I want to manage my account settings, including updating my profile, password, and email preferences.
Admin Interface

Story: As an admin, I want an intuitive dashboard to manage users, vendors, products, and orders.
Story: As an admin, I want to access detailed reports and analytics on the platformâ€™s performance.
These stories can be further broken down into tasks and sub-tasks during the development process.
